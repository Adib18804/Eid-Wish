Eid Mubarak!

May the blessings of Allah fill your life with joy and prosperity

تَقَبَّلَ اللهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم
"May Allah accept [good deeds] from us and from you"

As the crescent moon is sighted and the holy month of Ramadan comes to an end, may Allah shower His blessings upon you and your family. May this Eid bring happiness, peace, and prosperity to your life.

May Allah accept your fasting, forgive your sins, and guide you on the straight path. May He grant you the strength to stay steadfast in faith and fill your life with His infinite mercy and blessings. Eid Mubarak!

Mohammad Adib Abtahi
